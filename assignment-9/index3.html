How to Make a Paper Plane

Folding Instructions

Follow these steps to fold your paper plane:

  1. Start with a sheet of paper oriented in portrait mode.
  2. Fold the paper in half lengthwise, crease, and unfold.
  3. Fold the top edges to the center crease to form an isosceles triangle.
  4. Fold the triangle in half along the center crease.
  5. Fold the wings down to align with the bottom edge.
