Filipino chicken adobo


Ingredients

  • boneless skinless chicken thighs
    • cannot substitute with breast, need the fat to transform sauce into a glaze
  • soy sauce
  • white vinegar
  • onion and garlic
  • peppercornor coarsed cracked pepper
  • sugar
  • bay leaves
  • green onion

Directions

  1. Marinade for 20 minsPepper, soy sauce, sugar, onion and garlic
  2. Cook chicken in pan till brown
  3. Cook onion and garlic on pan till lightly brown
  4. Put onion on garlic and chicken on same pan then add 4 cups of soy sauce
  5. Add bayleaves, peppercorn, and a sprinkle of sugar
  6. Add 1 cup of water
    7. Cups of water will depend on how much sauce you want
  7. Cook till boil then add 4 cups of vinegar
  8. Cook and boil for 15-20 mins on medium heat
  9. Serve with rice!
